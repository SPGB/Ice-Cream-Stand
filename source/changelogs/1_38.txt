#PATCH 1.38 - August 22th

CORE
Added a real-time timer to see when you're able to vote next (as opposed to clicking to check)
Added an indicator of how close you are to completing a quest without clicking "complete quest" to check.
Increased the friend list size to 75

COW
Moved the hay spawn zones to be closer to the cow
Added item: backpack
Items are random drops alongside hay and rocks
Cows must now be purchased from a breeder.
Cows now grow and age.
Cows have the following stats: Strength (10-18), Constitution (10-18), Intelligence (10-18).
Strength determines how much of a bonus they give
Constitution determines the cows health
Intelligence determines how long a cow will stay happy
